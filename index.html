Quest Clicker!

How to Play

Click the chest to earn gold. Spend your gold in the shop to hire mercenaries and upgrade your weapons. The stronger your party and weapons, the faster you earn gold! Weapon upgrades increase gold per click, and allies give additonal clicks per second. More upgrades become accessible with more allies.

    Shop
  • Hire Mercenary — Automatically earns gold every second and unlocks a new hero to join your party. You can hire up to 11 mercenaries total.
  • Upgrade Sword — Increases the gold you earn per click by 1.
  • Upgrade Staff — Increases gold per click by 1. Only available after hiring your first mercenary.
  • Upgrade Dagger — Increases gold per click by 1.
  • Upgrade Potions — Increases gold per click by 1.
  • Upgrade Bow — Increases gold per click by 1.
  • Upgrade Lyre — Increases gold per click by 1.
